# Marketplace Recipes

Browse pre-built recipes — multi-step workflows that coordinate agents to complete complex tasks.

<!-- IMAGE: Marketplace Recipes tab showing recipe cards -->

## What you get

Each marketplace recipe includes:
- **Step definitions** — ordered list of tasks with agent assignments
- **Input parameters** — configurable variables
- **Schedule template** — suggested cron schedule (optional)
- **Description** — what the recipe does and when to use it

## Installing a recipe

1. Browse the Recipes tab
2. Click a recipe card to preview its steps
3. Click **Install**
4. The recipe appears in [Agents → Recipes](../agents/recipes.md)
5. Configure inputs and schedule as needed
6. Click **Run** to execute

{% hint style="info" %}
Recipes reference agent roles (e.g., "code reviewer"), not specific agents. The platform maps roles to your existing agents on install.
{% endhint %}

## Installing a recipe

1. Browse or search the Recipes tab
2. Click a recipe card to see details
3. Click **Install**
4. The recipe is copied to your workspace's recipe library
5. If the recipe references specific agent roles, you may need to map them to your own agents

{% hint style="info" %}
Installed recipes are independent copies. Updates to the marketplace version don't automatically update your installed copy.
{% endhint %}
